Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Texas at Austin in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE). I am working at the Laboratory for Image and Video Engineering (LIVE) under the guidance of Dr. Alan Bovik as a Graduate Research Assistant.

I am passionate about understanding the underlying statistics of images and videos and using them to develop robust and reliable computer vision, image and video processing systems.
